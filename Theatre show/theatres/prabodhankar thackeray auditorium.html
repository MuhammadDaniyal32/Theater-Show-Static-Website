Prabodhankar Thackeray Auditorium

Address: Chamunda Circle, Near Railway Station, Sodawala Lane, Borivali(W), Mumbai, Maharashtra 400091, India

Capacity: 1026


Probodhankar Thackeray Natya Mandir auditorium is a very popular event space in the Borivali West area of Mumbai. Over the years, this hall has played host to several plays, recitals, concerts and performances, especially in the Marathi entertainment industry. The spacious auditorium and hall space is perfect for large gatherings, where spectators can revel in the sights and sounds of dramatic enactments!

The complex offers an air-conditioned hall which is the ideal choice for functions held during the hot and humid months, as well as the rainy season. The auditorium/hall at Probodhankar Thackeray Natya Mandir is also a famous venue in the Borivali area for hosting large celebratory events such as weddings.

Kitchen facilities are provided by the venue for catering, which makes it easy for caterers and other vendors to set up, without disturbing guests in the main area. Many lavish pre-wedding functions like sangeets and engagements, private parties, festival and community events, as well as other social gatherings have been hosted at the Probodhankar Thackeray NatyaMandir in the last few years.
