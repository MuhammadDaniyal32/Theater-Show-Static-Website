Shanmukhananda Hall

Address: 292, Com., Harbanslal Marg, Sion East, Sion, Mumbai, Maharashtra 400022, India

Capacity: 2763


The main auditorium, renamed Shanmukhananda Chandrasekarendra Saraswathi Auditorium, is one of the largest of its kind in Asia. The main objective of the Sabha is to promote the fine arts. In pursuance of this, the Sabha has provided a platform for numerous artists to perform, over the years. Established, as well as budding artistes have performed here.

The stage dimensions are 30 ft. width (from side curtain to side curtain) x 20 ft. depth (from back curtain to front curtain), along with an outer area of 40 ft. (width) x 10 ft. (depth).



Seating

The total seating capacity is 2763. There are 1462 seats on the ground floor, 791 seats on the first floor and 510 seats on the second floor. The foyer is spacious. The lighting and sound systems are excellent.
