As one of India’s biggest stage for live-entertainment, Canvas Laugh Club is renowned for its stand-up shows. The company has been working with some of India’s finest comedic talent to present fresh content and has hence developed a religious following. Since 2010, CLC has treated Indian audiences to an art form that now, is becoming a big trend.

At India’s first dedicated venue for live comedy, every topic under the sun, whether controversial or light-hearted is brought to light, debated and laughed over. With comedians like AIB, East India Comedy, Anubhav Pal, Kanan Gill, Aditi Mittal, Varun Thakur, Abish Mathew and many more, we take pride in our obviously hilarious sense of humor.

The inception of Canvas Laugh Club can be traced back to the city of Mumbai, well before these Youtube sensations took over the comedy world. By hosting Open Mic for amateurs and fresh faces, comedy has received the much-required spotlight it always needed in India, especially amongst the millennials.

Apart from the stand-up comedy, Canvas Laugh Club makes for the ultimate destination to spend one's evening, to experience exquisite theatre performances like the Vagina Monologues, Improv Comedy, Children’s Theatre and much more
