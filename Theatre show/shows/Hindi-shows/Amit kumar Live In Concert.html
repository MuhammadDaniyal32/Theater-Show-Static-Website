Amit kumar Live In Concert

Prabodhankar Thackeray Auditorium

Symphony 7th - Sanjay Parab, Shreya Arts - Rajesh Thakkar & Bharat Waghela present "Amit Kumar Live in Concert on Saturday, 24th Nov. 2018 at 8.45 pm to 12 pm. at Prabodhankar Thackeray Auditorium, Borivali (W ) Mumbai. Amit Kumar will sing his superhit solo and duet songs.

He will also present Kishore Kumar solo and duet songs, sharing excellent memories of his father Kishore Kumar. Well-known and melodious female singers Soumya Varma and Priya Chawan will also perform with Amit Kumar. The concert will be hosted by R J Gaurav - Radio Jockey Fm City. The music will conduct by Anand Palwankar with 10 well-known Musicians.
