Life In Color India

Jawaharlal Nehru Stadium (Delhi)

Life in Color – The World’s Largest Paint party, features the world’s biggest DJ’s, soaring aerial acts, stilt walkers, contortionists, fire shows and the ever so famous paint blasts which give fans the ultimate music festival experience!

The renowned EDM festival is all set to happen in New Delhi on November 18th, 2018 at the Jawaharlal Nehru Stadium Grounds. At LIC our fans are the color to our canvas so we invite you to step out of reality and into our world of Life in Color!

Life in Color has had more than 500 successful shows in 30+ countries the highlights being the United States, United Kingdom, China, Thailand etc. Life in Color has been painting the world one city at a time since 2007 & has seen a total audience of one million in the process.

Life in color is almost like a Broadway show, but with such an energized crowd you would need to see it to believe it.
